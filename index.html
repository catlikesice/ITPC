What is ITPC?

The International Trusteeship for Peace and Coexistence (ITPC) is a proposed global governance initiative that seeks to place the territory of Israel and Palestine under a joint trusteeship administered by the United Kingdom, United Nations, European Union, China, and the Organization of Islamic Cooperation.

Our Objectives

  • Establish neutral international governance
  • Ensure religious freedom and protection of holy sites
  • Implement inclusive legal and civil systems
  • Promote repatriation, reconciliation, and redevelopment
  • Prepare for future self-determination through peaceful means
